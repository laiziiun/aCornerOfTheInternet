Minced beef and onions

Description

Recipe again courtesy of Dennis in stanford

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Onion
  • Korean bulgogi sauce
  • Garlic
  • Bit of oil

Instructions

  1. Ensure beef is fully thawed

  2. heat oil until hot, add garlic

  3. Add onions, stir until semi-transperant

  4. Add beef, cook until fully brown

  5. Pour bulgogi sauce when almost done
