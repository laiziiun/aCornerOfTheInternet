Ingredients

  • 500g chicken thigh for two serving
  • 1 onion
  • bunch of green onion
  • 4 eggs
  • rice
  • 0.5 spn hondashi (like jap buillion powder) / chicken salt
  • 1 cup water (330ml)
  • 3 tbspn light soy sauce
  • 3 tbspn mirin
  • 0.5 spn sugar
  • optional: red spice powder / bonito flakes

Instructions

  1. make sauce, bring half of it to boil

  2. add chicken and onions. bring to boil, simmer for 2 mins

  3. flip chicken, cook for another 4-5 mins

  4. Add eggs until desired cookness

  5. Suggest to cook one serving at a time to plate over rice. add chopped green onions

  6. can add red powder or bonito flakes
