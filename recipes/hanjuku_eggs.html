hanjuku eggs

Description

Half boiled eggs in soy sauce

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • optional: brown sugar or mirin
  • optional: Gomashio (mix of salt and sesame seeds)

Instructions

  1. bring water to a boil

  2. bring to simmer, add egg for 7 mins. stir ocassionally

  3. prep marinade: 1 part soya sauce to 3 parts water. pinch of sugar

  4. Once cooked, soak in marinade preferably overnight
