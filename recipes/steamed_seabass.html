Steamed seabass

Description

Recipe courtesy of Dennis.

Ingredients

  • frozen / fresh seabass
  • Soy sauce
  • Bit of brown sugar
  • Bit of ginger
  • Bit of green onion
  • Bit of sesame oil
  • Bit of normal cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Ensure fish is fully thawed

  2. Place water onto a boil

  3. Chop green onion and ginger into thin strips

  4. Steam fish for 9 mins on moderate heat

  5. Once steamed, remove essence from bowl

  6. Add sesame oil, soya sauce brown sugar onto fish

  7. Place green onion and ginger on fish, pour hot oil and serve
